<p><p>The Activated Carbon Tower market is categorized into two main types: Vertical Activated Carbon Tower and Horizontal Activated Carbon Tower. Vertical towers are designed to be installed in an upright position, allowing gravity to facilitate the downward flow of contaminants through the activated carbon bed. On the other hand, horizontal towers are installed horizontally, requiring a fan or blower to force air or gas through the carbon bed. Both types serve the purpose of efficiently removing impurities and odors from air or gas streams in various industrial applications.</p></p>
