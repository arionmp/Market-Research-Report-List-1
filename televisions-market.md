<p><p>Televisions, also known as TVs, are electronic devices used to display moving images along with accompanying sound. With individuals spending more time at home, there has been a surge in demand for TVs, as people seek entertainment options within their households. This trend is expected to continue even after the pandemic subsides.</p><p>In conclusion, the television market is expected to witness significant growth at a CAGR of 9% during the forecast period. Factors such as increasing disposable income, the popularity of smart TVs, and the adoption of UHD and 8K resolution displays are driving this growth.
