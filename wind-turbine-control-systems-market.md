<p><p>Wind Turbine Control Systems Market includes two main types of control cabinets, namely Tower Base Control Cabinet and Engine Room Control Cabinet. The Tower Base Control Cabinet is installed at the base of the wind turbine tower and is responsible for controlling various functions such as power conversion, voltage regulation, and system protection. On the other hand, the Engine Room Control Cabinet is situated in the engine room and is responsible for monitoring and controlling the operation of the wind turbine generator, providing real-time data, and ensuring optimal performance of the turbine.</p></p>
