<p><p>Industrial wireless in process industries refers to the use of wireless communication technologies in various market types. Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN) involves the use of a wireless network to connect devices within a limited area. Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN) enables communication between personal devices in close proximity. Satellite (GNSS) utilizes satellite systems for wireless communication over large distances. Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN) enables the transmission of small amounts of data over long distances with minimal power consumption. Cellular networks provide wireless connectivity using mobile network infrastructure. These market types facilitate efficient communication and data exchange in process industries.</p></p>
