<p><p>The print and apply labeling market consists of two main types: automatic labeling and semi-automatic labeling. In the automatic labeling market, the process of printing labels and applying them to products is fully automated. This technology requires minimal manual intervention and is suitable for high-volume production. On the other hand, in the semi-automatic labeling market, the process involves a combination of manual and automated steps. Operators are responsible for placing products and activating label printing and application, making it suitable for lower-volume production.</p></p>
