<p><p>The smart industrial camera market consists of two main types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture a two-dimensional image of a specific area in a single snapshot, making them suitable for applications such as quality control in manufacturing. On the other hand, line scan cameras capture a single line of pixels at a time but continuously scan the entire object, making them ideal for high-speed inspection of moving objects like conveyor belts. Both types of cameras play a crucial role in the automation and optimization of industrial processes.</p></p>
