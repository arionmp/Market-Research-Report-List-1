<p><p>The marine valves and actuators market consists of two main types: marine valves and marine actuators. Marine valves are devices used to control the flow of liquids or gases within a ship's piping system. They ensure proper functionality of various onboard systems such as cooling, fuel, and ballast. Marine actuators, on the other hand, are devices responsible for the automated movement or positioning of valves. They enable remote control and operation of marine valves, enhancing efficiency and safety in marine operations. Both marine valves and actuators play crucial roles in ensuring seamless functionality of ships' systems.</p></p>
