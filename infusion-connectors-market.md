<p><ul><li>Y Infusion Connector</li><li>T Infusion Connector</li><li>Straight Infusion Connector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infusion Connectors are an essential component in medical devices used for fluid delivery in the body. Y Infusion Connectors are designed in a Y-shape to allow multiple fluid lines to be connected. T Infusion Connectors are shaped like a T and are used to merge two fluid lines into one. Straight Infusion Connectors are simple connectors that allow a single fluid line to be connected. Each type of connector serves a specific purpose in medical procedures requiring precise fluid delivery.</p></p>
