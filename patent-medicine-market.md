<p><strong>The Patent Medicine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Product Patents</li><li>Process Patents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Patent medicine market types comprise product patents and process patents. Product patents grant exclusive rights to inventors for the creation of a particular medicine or drug. This prevents others from producing and selling the same medication using the same formula. Process patents, on the other hand, protect the specific method or technique used to manufacture the medicine. This ensures that other manufacturers cannot replicate the exact production process, maintaining the exclusivity of the original inventor in the market.</p></p>
