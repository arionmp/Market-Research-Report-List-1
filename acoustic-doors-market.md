<p><p>The acoustic doors market includes two main types: single doors and double doors. Single doors refer to a door unit with a single panel that is typically used for smaller openings. They are designed to reduce noise transmission and provide soundproofing in various settings such as theaters or recording studios. They are commonly utilized for wider openings or spaces that require larger sound barriers, like conference rooms or concert halls.</p></p>
