<p><p>LVT (Luxury Vinyl Tile) Flooring is divided into two main market types: Flexible LVT Flooring and Rigid LVT Flooring. Flexible LVT Flooring is composed of multiple layers and has a pliable surface that is easy to install and provides cushioning. On the other hand, Rigid LVT Flooring is constructed with a stiffer core layer, making it more durable and resistant to imperfections in the subfloor. Both options offer the benefits of durability, water resistance, and easy maintenance, but differ in their level of flexibility and rigidity.</p></p>
