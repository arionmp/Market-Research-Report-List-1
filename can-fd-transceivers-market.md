<p><p>CAN FD (Controller Area Network with Flexible Data Rate) transceivers are used in automotive and industrial applications for efficient and reliable communication between electronic control units (ECUs). The market for CAN FD transceivers can be categorized into two types: isolated and non-isolated. Isolated transceivers provide galvanic isolation between the CAN bus and the transceiver, protecting the system from voltage spikes and noise. Non-isolated transceivers do not offer galvanic isolation and are commonly used when isolation is not required or can be provided by other components in the system.</p></p>
