<p><p>Modular commercial building refers to the construction of commercial buildings using prefabricated modules or off-site manufactured components that are later assembled on-site. <p><p>The modular commercial building market consists of two main types: permanent and relocatable buildings. Permanent buildings are constructed to be a fixed and durable structure at a specific location, serving long-term purposes such as offices, schools, or healthcare facilities. On the other hand, relocatable buildings are designed to be easily disassembled, moved, and reassembled in different locations. These buildings offer flexibility and can serve temporary needs like construction site offices, event spaces, or pop-up shops. Both types of modular buildings provide efficient and cost-effective solutions for various commercial requirements.</p></p>
