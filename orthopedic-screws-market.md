<p><p>Orthopedic screws are medical devices used in orthopedic surgeries to fix bones and joints. These screws are designed to provide stability and proper alignment of fractured bones or to hold bones together during the healing process. Orthopedic screws are made of various materials such as stainless steel, titanium, and bioabsorbable materials.</p><p>The global orthopedic screws market has been experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding. Factors driving market growth include the rising prevalence of orthopedic disorders and injuries, increasing geriatric population, and advancements in surgical technologies. Additionally, the growing demand for minimally invasive surgeries and the rising adoption of orthopedic implants further propel market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, several trends have been observed in the orthopedic screws market. One major trend is the increasing adoption of bioabsorbable screws due to their biocompatibility and ability to be absorbed by the body over time. These screws eliminate the need for a second surgery for screw removal, reducing patient discomfort and healthcare costs.</p><p>Another trend is the development of patient-specific orthopedic screws. With the advancements in 3D printing technology, orthopedic screws can now be customized to fit the patient's unique anatomy, offering improved surgical outcomes.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing a shift towards outpatient surgeries, driven by the growing preference for minimally invasive procedures. Outpatient surgeries reduce hospital stays, lower healthcare costs, and enable quicker recovery, thus driving the demand for orthopedic screws.</p><p>In conclusion, the global orthopedic screws market is projected to witness significant growth in the coming years. Factors such as the increasing prevalence of orthopedic disorders, advancements in surgical technologies, and the rising adoption of minimally invasive surgeries contribute to this growth. The trend towards bioabsorbable screws and patient-specific orthopedic screws further enhance market potential. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.7% during the forecast period.</p></p>
