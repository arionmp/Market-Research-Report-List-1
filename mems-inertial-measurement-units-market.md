<p><p>MEMS inertial measurement units (IMUs) are devices that measure and track an object's motion, orientation, and positioning. The market types of IMUs include 4DoF (Degrees of Freedom), 6DoF, 9DoF, 10DoF, and other categories. </p><p>4DoF IMUs provide basic motion sensing capabilities in three axes: pitch, roll, and yaw. </p><p>6DoF IMUs add three additional degrees of freedom by including linear accelerations in each axis, enabling precise motion tracking. </p><p>9DoF IMUs integrate a magnetometer to detect the Earth's magnetic field, allowing for accurate heading determination.</p><p>10DoF IMUs also include a barometer, enabling altitude measurement in addition to motion tracking.</p><p>The "other" market segment encompasses IMUs with unique or specialized features beyond the mentioned DoF configurations.</p></p>
