<p><p>The Manual Wire Bonders market comprises three main types: Ball-Wedge Manual Wire Bonders, Wedge-Wedge Manual Wire Bonders, and Convertible Manual Wire Bonders. Ball-Wedge bonders use a combination of ball and wedge bonding techniques to create reliable wire connections. Wedge-Wedge bonders utilize the wedge bonding technique exclusively. Convertible bonders offer the flexibility to switch between ball and wedge bonding methods as per the requirements of the application. These wire bonders are manual in nature, meaning they require human operation for wire bonding processes.</p></p>
