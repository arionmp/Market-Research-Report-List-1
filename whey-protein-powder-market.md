<p><p>The whey protein powder market can be categorized into three types: whey protein concentrate powder, whey protein isolate powder, and whey protein hydrolysate powder. Whey protein concentrate powder is the most common type and contains a relatively high level of protein and some lactose and fat. Whey protein isolate powder, on the other hand, has undergone additional processing to remove lactose and fat, resulting in a higher protein content. Lastly, whey protein hydrolysate powder is pre-digested, making it easier to absorb by the body.</p></p>
