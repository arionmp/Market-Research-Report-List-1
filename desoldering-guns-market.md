<p><p>Desoldering guns are handheld tools used to remove solder from electronic components during the repair or rework process. They are commonly used in industries such as electronics manufacturing, telecommunications, aerospace, and automotive, where the removal and replacement of electronic components are frequent.</p><p>Desoldering guns typically feature a heating element that quickly heats up the solder, making it melt and allowing easy removal. Some guns also have a built-in vacuum pump or suction system that assists in removing the molten solder. They provide a quick and efficient way to desolder components without causing damage to the surrounding parts.</p><p>The desoldering guns market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to the increasing demand for electronic devices and the need for repair and maintenance services.
