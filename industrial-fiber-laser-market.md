<p><strong>The Industrial Fiber Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Fiber Laser Market Types:</p><p>1. Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser: Continuous Wave fiber lasers emit a continuous beam of laser energy. They are used for applications that require a constant laser output, such as material cutting, welding, and drilling. CW fiber lasers are known for their high power, excellent beam quality, and long operational lifetime.</p><p>2. Pulsed Fiber Laser: Pulsed fiber lasers emit laser energy in short pulses. These lasers are suitable for tasks that require high peak powers, such as marking, engraving, and micromachining. Pulsed fiber lasers offer precise control over the pulse duration and high pulse repetition rates, enabling fast and accurate processing of materials.</p></p>
