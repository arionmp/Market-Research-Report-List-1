<p><p>The Linear Lighting Market encompasses various types of lighting options. Static White lighting provides a fixed and consistent white light color temperature. Tunable White lighting allows users to adjust the color temperature, ranging from warm to cool white light. RGB lighting uses red, green, and blue color channels to create a wide range of vibrant colors. RGBW lighting adds a separate white channel to the RGB system, enabling a more precise and accurate white light. Lastly, the "Others" category includes unique lighting options that do not fit into the aforementioned types.</p></p>
