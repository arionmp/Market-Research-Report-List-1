<p><p>Oil containment boom is a vital equipment used to prevent the spread of oil spills in water bodies such as oceans, rivers, and harbors. It is typically deployed in emergency response situations to contain and control oil spills, protecting sensitive coastal areas and wildlife habitats.</p><p>The current outlook of the oil containment boom market is positive and is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
