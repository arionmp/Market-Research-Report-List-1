Copper Cable refers to the use of copper wires to transmit data signals. Fiber Cable involves the use of optical fibers to transmit data through light signals. Copper Components encompass the various elements required for a copper-based cabling system, such as connectors and patch panels. Fiber Components comprise the necessary components for a fiber-based cabling system, including connectors and couplers. These types form the foundation of the Structured Cabling market for data transmission and network connectivity.</p></p>
