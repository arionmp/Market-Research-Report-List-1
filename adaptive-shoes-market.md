<p><p>Adaptive shoes, also known as orthopedic or therapeutic shoes, are specially designed footwear that provides comfort, support, and relief for individuals with foot-related medical conditions or mobility issues. The rising prevalence of chronic diseases like diabetes, arthritis, and foot deformities is also driving the demand for adaptive shoes. Furthermore, technological advancements in shoe manufacturing, such as 3D printing and advanced materials, are enabling the development of more comfortable and customizable adaptive shoes.</p><p>The future of the adaptive shoes market looks promising as the demand for these specialized footwear is projected to increase.
