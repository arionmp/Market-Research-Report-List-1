<p><strong>The Photonic Infrared Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared</li><li>Mid Wave Infrared</li><li>Long Wave Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photonic infrared sensors market consists of three main types: Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR sensors operate in the wavelength range of 0.9 to 1.7 micrometers, providing low resolution but high sensitivity images. MWIR sensors work in the 3 to 5 micrometers range, offering slightly higher resolution and detecting warm objects. LWIR sensors operate at wavelengths of 8 to 14 micrometers, providing high-resolution images and detecting even cooler objects. These sensor types cater to various applications in industries like defense, aerospace, and surveillance.</p></p>
