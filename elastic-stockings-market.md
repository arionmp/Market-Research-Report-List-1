<p><p>Elastic stockings come in various types, including gradient socks and anti-embolism socks. Gradient socks have graduated compression, meaning they provide stronger compression at the ankle and gradually decrease towards the knee. These socks aid in improving blood circulation, reducing swelling, and preventing varicose veins. Anti-embolism socks, on the other hand, are specifically designed to prevent blood clots in patients who are immobilized or undergoing surgery. They apply gentle pressure to the legs, helping to maintain proper blood flow and minimize the risk of clot formation.</p></p>
