<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode</li><li>Single-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Vibration Sensing (DVS) market includes two main types: Multi-Mode and Single-Mode systems. Multi-Mode DVS utilizes multiple fibers for sensing vibrations along a continuous length, allowing for comprehensive monitoring of large areas, such as oil pipelines or rail tracks. It provides real-time data on various parameters, enhancing accuracy and reliability. On the other hand, Single-Mode DVS operates using only a single fiber for vibration detection, making it suitable for smaller-scale applications like perimeter security or structural health monitoring. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on the specific monitoring requirements.</p></p>
