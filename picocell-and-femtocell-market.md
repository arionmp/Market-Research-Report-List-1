<p><p>Picocells and femtocells are types of small-scale cellular base stations used to improve mobile network coverage and capacity in specific areas. They are designed to provide enhanced signal strength and data transmission capabilities in areas with high user density or poor cellular coverage.</p><p>Picocells are larger and have a higher power output compared to femtocells. They are typically used to cover larger indoor spaces or outdoor areas such as shopping malls, stadiums, and campuses. On the other hand, femtocells are smaller and intended for residential or small office use, offering coverage within a range of a few hundred meters.</p><p>The global picocell and femtocell market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. Factors such as increasing mobile data traffic, the need for better indoor coverage, and the growing demand for seamless connectivity have been driving the market growth. The market is also fueled by the continuous technological advancements in wireless communication and the deployment of 5G networks.</p><p>Moreover, the market has witnessed various trends that are shaping its growth. One such trend is the rising adoption of femtocells by enterprises to improve in-building wireless coverage and support enterprise applications.
