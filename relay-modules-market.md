<p><strong>The Relay Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Switching</li><li>Electromechanical</li><li>DC</li><li>Solid-state</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Relay modules are used in various applications to control or switch electrical circuits. The switching relay module is the most common type, allowing the control of large currents or voltages. Electromechanical relay modules use an electromagnetic coil to open or close a circuit. DC modules are specifically designed to handle direct current circuits. Solid-state relay modules use semiconductors instead of mechanical parts to achieve switching. Other types of relay modules may include reed relays, optocouplers, or time delay relays, each offering specific functionalities based on the application requirements.</p></p>
