<p><p>The photoresist coater market can be classified into two types: automatic and semi-automatic. An automatic photoresist coater is a fully automated machine that requires minimal human intervention. It can perform tasks such as dispensing the photoresist material, coating it onto substrates, and adjusting parameters automatically. On the other hand, a semi-automatic photoresist coater requires some level of human intervention, such as manually loading substrates into the machine and adjusting certain settings. Both types cater to different customer preferences and requirements in terms of automation and ease of operation.</p></p>
