<p><p>Low-temperature freezers are available in three main types: chest, cabinet, and built-in. Chest freezers are popular for their large storage capacity and efficient cooling, making them ideal for long-term preservation of samples and medications. Cabinet freezers are more compact and suitable for easy access to frequently used items in laboratories and medical facilities. Built-in freezers are designed to be integrated into existing cabinetry, offering a discreet and space-saving solution for storage of sensitive materials. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the low-temperature freezer market.</p></p>
