<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The laboratory sterilizers market is divided into four types based on the sterilization method used. Heat sterilizers use high temperatures to kill microorganisms. Low-temperature sterilizers employ chemicals like hydrogen peroxide or ethylene oxide gas to eliminate pathogens. Ionizing radiation sterilizers utilize gamma rays or electron beams to destroy microorganisms. Filtration sterilizers remove bacteria and viruses by passing the samples through filters. These sterilization methods play a crucial role in ensuring that laboratory equipment, supplies, and samples are free from harmful microorganisms.</p></p>
