<p><p>Hotel Internet Booking Engine (IBE) market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and web-based. Cloud-based IBEs utilize remote servers, allowing hotels to access and manage their booking systems online without the need for local installations. They offer scalability, easy updates, and data security. On the other hand, web-based IBEs require on-premise installations and are restricted to the hotel's physical location. They offer fewer scalability options and require regular manual updates, but can provide more control over data security and customization.</p></p>
