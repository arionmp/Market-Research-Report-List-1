<p><p>Stitching machines can be categorized into two types: manually operated and electrically operated. Manually operated machines require the user to manually control the stitching process by using a hand or foot pedal. These machines are often preferred for small-scale stitching tasks and simple designs. On the other hand, electrically operated machines are powered by electricity and offer automated stitching capabilities. They are suitable for large-scale production, complex designs, and faster stitching speeds. The choice between these two types depends on the specific requirements and volume of work in different industries.</p></p>
