<p><strong>The Photovoltaic Backsheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluoropolymer</li><li>Non-Fluoropolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic backsheet market can be classified into two types: fluoropolymer and non-fluoropolymer. Fluoropolymer backsheets are made from materials like polyvinyl fluoride (PVF) or fluorinated ethylene propylene (FEP), which provide excellent durability, weather resistance, and insulation properties. Non-fluoropolymer backsheets, on the other hand, are made from materials like polyethylene terephthalate (PET) or polyamide (PA), which offer good mechanical strength and value for money. Both types of backsheets play a crucial role in protecting the solar cells from environmental factors and ensuring the overall performance and longevity of photovoltaic modules.</p></p>
