<p><p>The medical disinfectant gel market is divided into three main types: alcohol disinfectant gel, quaternary ammonium salt disinfection gel, and chlorine disinfectant gel. Alcohol disinfectant gel contains ethyl or isopropyl alcohol and is effective against a wide range of pathogens. Quaternary ammonium salt disinfection gel utilizes ammonium compounds to kill microorganisms. Chlorine disinfectant gel contains chlorine compounds and is commonly used for disinfecting surfaces. These different types of disinfectant gels cater to various needs in the medical industry, ensuring proper hygiene and reducing the spread of infections.</p></p>
