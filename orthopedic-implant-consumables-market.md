<p><p>Orthopedic implant consumables are surgical instruments used in orthopedic implant surgeries to repair or replace damaged bones and joints. Firstly, the increasing geriatric population, which is more susceptible to bone-related disorders such as arthritis and osteoporosis, is driving the demand for orthopedic implants and consumables. Additionally, the rising prevalence of sports injuries and road accidents is contributing to the market growth as these often require orthopedic implant surgeries. </p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology have led to the development of innovative implant materials and designs, resulting in improved surgical outcomes and patient satisfaction. For instance, the introduction of 3D-printed implants has revolutionized the orthopedic industry. These factors are further propelling the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing adoption of minimally invasive surgeries, which require smaller incisions and result in faster recovery, is driving the demand for orthopedic implant consumables. These procedures have gained popularity due to their advantages over traditional open surgeries, such as reduced pain, shorter hospital stays, and lower risk of complications.</p><p>According to market analysis, the orthopedic implant consumables market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period.
