<p><p>Extrusion heads are crucial components in the plastic extrusion process. There are two main types of extrusion heads: multilayer and single-layer. Multilayer extrusion heads are used to produce plastic products with multiple layers, each providing different properties and functionalities. This ensures enhanced strength, durability, and flexibility in the final product. On the other hand, single-layer extrusion heads are designed to create plastic products with a single layer, suitable for applications that do not require specialized properties. The choice between these two types depends on the specific requirements of the desired plastic product.</p></p>
