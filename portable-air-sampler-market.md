<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Portable Air Sampler market is categorized into two types: Active Type and Passive Type. The Active Type sampler operates by actively drawing in air samples using a pump or fan mechanism. It is suitable for collecting samples at a specific flow rate and is often used for quantitative analysis. On the other hand, the Passive Type sampler collects air samples without the use of a pump or fan, relying on natural diffusion processes. It is typically used for qualitative analysis or long-term monitoring where continuous air movement is not necessary.</p></p>
