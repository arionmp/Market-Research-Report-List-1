<p><p>The luggage conveyor market encompasses different types of conveyor systems used for handling luggage at airports, hotels, and other travel hubs. Belt conveyors are a common type, utilizing a continuous belt to move luggage smoothly. Roller conveyors feature rotating rollers that facilitate the movement of luggage along a fixed path. Other types include conveyor belts with built-in scales for weighing luggage and specialized conveyors for oversized or irregularly shaped items. These various market types offer different functionalities and cater to specific needs in the luggage handling industry.</p></p>
