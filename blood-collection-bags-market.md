<p><p>Blood collection bags are used in healthcare settings to collect and store blood donations for transfusion or further testing. There are four main types of blood collection bags: single collection bags, double collection bags, triple collection bags, and quadruple collection bags. Single collection bags are used to collect and store a single unit of blood. Double collection bags have two compartments for collecting two different blood components, such as red cells and plasma. Triple collection bags have three compartments for collecting three different blood components. Finally, quadruple collection bags have four compartments for collecting four different blood components.</p></p>
