<p><p>Portable projectors are devices used to project images or videos onto a screen or wall. There are two main types: DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) projectors. DLP projectors use tiny mirrors to reflect light and create images, offering high contrast and smooth motion. LCD projectors, on the other hand, use liquid crystal panels to display images, providing vibrant colors and high brightness. Both types have their own advantages and are popular choices in the portable projector market.</p></p>
