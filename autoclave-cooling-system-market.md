<p><p>Autoclave cooling systems can be classified into two main types: automatic and non-automatic. Automatic systems are designed to regulate the cooling process without human intervention. They use sensors and advanced technology to monitor temperature and pressure, ensuring efficient and safe cooling. On the other hand, non-automatic systems require manual adjustment and monitoring by operators. They lack the advanced features of automatic systems but are often more affordable. The choice between these two types depends on factors such as budget, desired level of control, and the complexity of cooling requirements.</p></p>
