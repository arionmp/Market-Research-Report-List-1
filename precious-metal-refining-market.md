<p><p>The precious metal refining market consists of three major types: Silver (Ag), Gold (Au), and Platinum Group Metals (PGMs). Silver refining involves the extraction and purification of silver from various sources such as ores, electronic waste, and jewelry scrap. Gold refining focuses on the purification and separation of gold from ores, old jewelry, and electronic components. PGM refining mainly deals with platinum, palladium, rhodium, iridium, ruthenium, and osmium extraction from automobile catalytic converters and electronic waste. All three markets play a crucial role in providing valuable and highly sought-after metals for various industries.</p></p>
