<p><p>The Digital Excitation Control System (DECS) market consists of various types including Static Excitation System, Brushless Excitation System, and Others. Static Excitation Systems use solid-state components to control the output of the excitation system, providing accurate voltage regulation and rapid response. Brushless Excitation Systems eliminate the need for brushes and commutators, offering improved reliability and reduced maintenance. The Others category includes alternative excitation control systems such as rotating diode and rotating transformer excitation systems. These systems play a crucial role in regulating the output of power generators and ensuring stable electrical power supply.</p></p>
