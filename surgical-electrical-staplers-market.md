<p><p>The Surgical Electrical Staplers Market is divided into two types: Disposable and Reusable. Disposable staplers are designed for single-use, meaning they are used on one patient and then disposed of. They offer convenience, reduced risk of cross-contamination, and eliminate the need for reprocessing. On the other hand, reusable staplers can be used multiple times after proper sterilization. They are cost-effective in the long run but require additional resources for reprocessing and carry a slightly higher risk of cross-contamination if not handled and sterilized properly.</p></p>
