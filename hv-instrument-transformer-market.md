<p><p>The HV instrument transformer market includes three main types: current transformers, voltage transformers, and others. Current transformers are used to measure and transform high currents into lower values for protection and metering purposes. Voltage transformers, also known as potential transformers, are employed to step down high voltages to safer levels for instrumentation and protective devices. The 'others' category includes specialty instrument transformers like combined current-voltage transformers, which serve unique applications. All these instrument transformers play a crucial role in the power system by ensuring accurate measurement and efficient protection of electrical equipment.</p></p>
