<p><p>The compact carry gun market consists of two main types: automatic and manual. Automatic compact carry guns, also known as semi-automatics, utilize a firing mechanism that automatically reloads a new bullet into the chamber after each shot is fired. These guns are popular for their ease of use and quick follow-up shots. On the other hand, manual compact carry guns, such as revolvers, require the user to manually reload each round into the chamber by either swinging out the cylinder or using a speed loader. These guns are appreciated for their reliability and simplicity.</p></p>
