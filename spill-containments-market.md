<p><p>Spill containment market is divided into different types, including absorbent and others. The absorbent market focuses on products specifically designed to soak up spills and prevent them from spreading. These products typically include absorbent pads, booms, pillows, and socks made from materials such as polypropylene or cellulose. On the other hand, the "others" segment encompasses alternative spill containment solutions, such as secondary containment systems, spill berms, and drain protection products. These alternative options aim to prevent spills from reaching the environment by creating barriers or diverting them to designated areas for proper disposal.</p></p>
