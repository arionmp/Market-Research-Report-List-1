CCD sensors have traditionally been known for their high-quality image output and low noise levels. However, CMOS sensors have gained popularity over time due to their improved energy efficiency, faster readout speeds, and better performance in low light conditions. Both CCD and CMOS sensors cater to different photography needs and preferences in the ILC market.</p></p>
