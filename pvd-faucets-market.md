<p><p>PVD faucets, also known as Physical Vapor Deposition faucets, are a type of faucet coating that enhances its durability and appearance. This results in a highly resilient and corrosion-resistant coating that not only gives the faucet a modern and sleek look but also extends its lifespan.</p><p>The PVD faucets market is experiencing steady growth due to the increasing demand for durable and aesthetically pleasing bathroom and kitchen fixtures.
