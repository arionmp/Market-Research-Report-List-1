Raw honey is a sweet substance produced by bees from the nectar of flowers. Blackstrap molasses is a byproduct obtained during the sugar refining process. Other natural sweeteners include maple syrup, agave nectar, and monk fruit extract. These alternatives offer healthier options for consumers seeking to reduce their intake of artificial sweeteners.</p></p>
