In the low-frequency market, transformers are designed to operate at frequencies below 2 kHz, typically used in power transmission and distribution systems. Intermediate-frequency transformers operate in the frequency range of 2 kHz to 30 kHz and are commonly utilized in applications like welding machines. High-frequency transformers are optimized for frequencies above 30 kHz and find applications in industries like telecommunications and electronics. These market types are distinguished by their frequency range and application domains.</p></p>
