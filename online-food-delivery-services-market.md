<p><p>Online food delivery services can be categorized into two main types: restaurant-to-consumer and platform-to-consumer markets. In the restaurant-to-consumer market, customers place their orders directly with a specific restaurant, which then processes and delivers the food. On the other hand, in the platform-to-consumer market, customers place their orders through an online platform that collaborates with multiple restaurants. The platform handles the processing and delivery of the orders, providing customers with access to various restaurant options through a single platform.</p></p>
