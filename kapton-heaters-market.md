<p><p>Kapton heaters are categorized into two types based on their shape: Rectangle type and Round type. Rectangle type Kapton heaters are designed in a rectangular shape, making them suitable for applications that require a larger surface area. They are commonly used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics. On the other hand, Round type Kapton heaters are circular in shape and are typically used in applications where a more concentrated heat is required. Both types of Kapton heaters are widely used and offer efficient heating solutions in various industries.</p></p>
