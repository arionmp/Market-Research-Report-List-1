The Wire Type Spirometer requires a physical connection between the spirometer device and the PC through a cable. This type offers reliable data transfer but limits the user's mobility. On the other hand, the Wireless Type Spirometer allows for wireless connectivity between the device and the PC, enabling greater freedom of movement for the user. While it may have slightly lower data transfer stability, it offers convenience and flexibility.</p></p>
