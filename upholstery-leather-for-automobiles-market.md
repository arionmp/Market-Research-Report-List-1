<p><p>The upholstery leather for automobiles market consists of two primary types: genuine leather and synthetic leather. Genuine leather is made from animal hides, typically cowhide, and is known for its durability, natural look, and luxurious feel. It is more expensive than synthetic leather but is highly regarded for its quality and longevity. Synthetic leather, on the other hand, is made from artificial materials such as PVC or polyurethane. It offers a wide range of colors and textures, and is often more affordable than genuine leather. However, it may not have the same durability or premium feel as genuine leather.</p></p>
