<p><p>Virtual Data Optimizer (VDO) is a software solution that provides data compression and deduplication capabilities for virtual infrastructures and storage systems. This enables organizations to significantly improve data storage and reduce the cost of storage systems. By removing duplicate or similar data, VDO improves the overall performance of virtual environments by reducing the amount of data that needs to be processed, transferred, and stored.</p><p>According to market research, the Virtual Data Optimizer market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
