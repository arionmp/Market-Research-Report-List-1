<p><p>The Airport Friction Tester market is comprised of three main types: Vehicle-mount, Towed, and Walk-behind testers. Vehicle-mount testers are installed on specialized vehicles and measure the friction levels of airport runways while driving at normal speeds. Towed testers are pulled by a vehicle and provide measurements while being towed along the runway. Walk-behind testers are portable and require an operator to manually walk along the runway to collect friction data. All three types serve the purpose of assessing runway conditions for safe aircraft operations.</p></p>
