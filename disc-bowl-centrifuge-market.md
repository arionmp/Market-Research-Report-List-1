<p><p>The disc bowl centrifuge market can be categorized into two main types: liquid-liquid separation and liquid-solid separation markets. In the liquid-liquid separation market, the centrifuge is used to separate two immiscible liquids with different densities, such as oil and water. It achieves separation by creating a force that causes the heavier liquid to move towards the periphery while the lighter liquid concentrates in the center. On the other hand, in the liquid-solid separation market, the centrifuge is employed to separate solid particles from a liquid suspension, enabling the recovery of valuable solids or the clarification of the liquid.</p></p>
