<p><strong>The EC Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Motor</li><li>Outer Rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EC motors, or electronically commutated motors, are widely used in various industries due to their energy efficiency and precise speed control. Two main types of EC motors are inner rotor motors and outer rotor motors. </p><p>Inner rotor motors have the rotor (rotating part) located within the stator (stationary part). This arrangement offers compactness and high power density, making them suitable for applications with limited space. </p><p>On the other hand, outer rotor motors have the rotor placed outside the stator. This design provides higher torque and efficiency, making them commonly used in applications that require higher power requirements and continuous operation.</p></p>
