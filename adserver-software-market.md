<p><p>The Adserver Software Market can be classified into two types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise. Cloud-Based adserver software operates on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage their advertising campaigns through the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as no physical infrastructure is required. On the other hand, On-Premise adserver software is installed and operated on local servers, providing users with full control and customization options. However, it often involves higher upfront costs and requires maintenance and updates by the user's IT team.</p></p>
