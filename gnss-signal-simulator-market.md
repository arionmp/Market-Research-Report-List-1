<p><strong>The GNSS Signal Simulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel Simulator</li><li>Multi Channel Simulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GNSS Signal Simulator market includes two types: Single Channel Simulator and Multi Channel Simulator. Single Channel Simulators are designed to generate signals for a single satellite system, such as GPS or GLONASS. They are simpler, more affordable, and suitable for applications that only require signals from one system. On the other hand, Multi Channel Simulators can generate multiple signals from different satellite systems simultaneously, like GPS, GLONASS, and Galileo. These simulators offer more versatility and flexibility for a wide range of applications requiring signals from multiple systems.</p></p>
