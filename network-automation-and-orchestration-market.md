<p><p>Network automation and orchestration refer to the process of automating and managing network operations, configurations, and services through software-based techniques and tools. This approach eliminates the need for manual configuration and allows networks to adapt to changing business needs automatically.</p><p>Overall, the Network Automation and Orchestration Market is poised for substantial growth as businesses increasingly recognize the benefits of automating and optimizing network operations.</p></p>
