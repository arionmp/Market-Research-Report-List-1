<p><p>The initiative sensor market consists of two main types: single beam type and multi-beam type. A single beam type sensor emits a single beam of light or soundwave and measures the time it takes for the beam to reflect back. This allows it to detect objects and determine their distance or presence. On the other hand, a multi-beam type sensor emits multiple beams and can provide more accurate and detailed measurements by detecting and analyzing the reflections from different angles. Both types have their own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
