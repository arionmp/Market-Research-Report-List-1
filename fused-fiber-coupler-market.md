<p><p>The Fused Fiber Coupler market can be categorized into two types: Single Mode Couplers and Multimode Couplers. Single Mode Couplers are designed to align the light beams in a fiber optic system, allowing efficient transmission of data signals. They are suitable for long-distance communication and high-speed networks. On the other hand, Multimode Couplers are used for shorter distance communication and lower speed networks. They can combine or divide multiple signals simultaneously. Both types of couplers play crucial roles in ensuring reliable and effective data transmission in different applications.</p></p>
