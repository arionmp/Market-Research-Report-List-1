<p><p>The ECG electrodes market can be categorized based on conducting medium. Liquid electrodes are filled with conductive gel and offer good signal quality but can be messy. Solid-gel electrodes have a gel layer that reduces movement artifacts but may cause skin irritation. The market can also be segmented based on back-lining materials. Sponge back-lining provides better adhesion and comfort, while non-woven back-lining offers cost-effective options. These different types cater to different user preferences and requirements in terms of signal quality, comfort, and cost.</p></p>
