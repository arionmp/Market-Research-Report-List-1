<p><p>Air Data Calibration Test Systems (ADCTS) are used to calibrate and test air data instruments such as pitot-static probes and altimeters. The market for ADCTS can be categorized into three types: portable type, table top, and mounted type. </p><p>Portable ADCTS are compact and easy to transport, making them suitable for field calibration and test purposes. Table top ADCTS are larger systems that are designed to be placed on a table or workbench for calibration and testing in a laboratory setting. Mounted ADCTS are permanently installed in a specific location, such as an aircraft maintenance facility, for regular calibration and testing of air data instruments.</p></p>
