<p><p>Luminaire and lighting control markets can be categorized into two types: wired and wireless. In wired lighting control systems, devices are connected via physical cables for communication and power supply. This type offers stability and reliability but may require more installation time and cost. On the other hand, wireless lighting control systems use radio frequency or infrared signals for communication, providing flexibility and ease of installation. These systems are typically easier to expand and reconfigure, although they may be susceptible to interference.</p></p>
