The increasing adoption of telepresence solutions in various sectors, such as healthcare, education, and corporate enterprises, is driving market growth. It offers a collaborative and immersive communication experience. </p><p>Personal telepresence equipment provides an individualized video conferencing solution that can be used from any location, typically through personal devices like desktop computers or laptops. It offers flexibility and convenience for remote communication.</p><p>Room-based telepresence equipment is designed for larger groups or conference rooms, providing a more immersive experience with high-quality audio and video. It enables seamless communication between multiple locations and enhances collaboration.</p></p>
