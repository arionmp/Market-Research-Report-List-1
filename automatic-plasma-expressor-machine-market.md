<p><strong>The Automatic Plasma Expressor Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Press</li><li>Double Press</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Plasma Expressor Machine market is categorized into two types: Single Press and Double Press. A Single Press machine works by applying pressure on the plasma bag once, extracting the plasma from the bag and separating it from the other components. On the other hand, a Double Press machine applies pressure twice, further squeezing the bag to extract more plasma. Both types of machines are used for plasma collection in medical facilities but differ in the amount of plasma they are able to extract from a single bag.</p></p>
