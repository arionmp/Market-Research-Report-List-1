<p><strong>The Indoor Positioning And Navigation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UWB</li><li>Wi-Fi</li><li>Bluetooth Low Energy</li><li>RFID</li><li>CellularBased</li><li>Lo-Ra</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Indoor positioning and navigation systems consist of various market types. Ultra-Wideband (UWB) technology uses low-power radio waves to measure distance between devices accurately. Wi-Fi enables positioning and navigation using existing Wi-Fi infrastructure. Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) utilizes short-range wireless technology for indoor tracking. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) relies on electromagnetic fields for identifying and tracking tags attached to objects. Cellular-based systems employ mobile network signals for indoor positioning. Lo-Ra (Long Range) technology enables long-distance communication for low-power devices, suitable for large indoor areas. These market types provide diverse options for indoor positioning and navigation solutions.</p></p>
