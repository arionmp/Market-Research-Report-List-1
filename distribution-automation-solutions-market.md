<p><ul><li>System-Level Distribution Automation Solutions</li><li>Customer-Level Distribution Automation Solutions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distribution Automation Solutions (DAS) can be categorized into two types: System-Level DAS and Customer-Level DAS. System-Level DAS refers to the automation and optimization of various components of the electricity distribution system, such as substations, transformers, and distribution lines. It aims to enhance the overall efficiency and reliability of the distribution system. On the other hand, Customer-Level DAS focuses on delivering improved services and functionalities directly to the consumers, including smart meters and home energy management systems. Both types play a crucial role in modernizing and enhancing the performance of the electricity distribution network.</p></p>
