<p><p>Ionisation chambers are widely used in radiation monitoring and dosimetry. The market for ionisation chambers can be categorized into different types based on their design and application. Cylindrical ionisation chambers have a tubular shape and are commonly used in medical radiation therapy. Parallel plate ionisation chambers consist of two parallel metal plates and are often employed in industrial radiography. Thimble type ionisation chambers have a cylindrical shape with a central electrode probe, primarily used for measuring environmental radiation. Other types of ionisation chambers include pocket dosimeters and survey meters, which have compact designs for personal radiation monitoring.</p></p>
