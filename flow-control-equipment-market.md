<p><p>Flow control equipment market types are categorized into three main categories: automatic control valve, semi-automatic control valve, and manual control valve. </p><p>Automatic control valves are designed to regulate flow without any human intervention, as they are set to respond automatically to changes in pressure or flow rate. </p><p>Semi-automatic control valves require some human input to adjust the flow, but they also have certain automatic features for controlling flow.</p><p>Manual control valves, on the other hand, rely entirely on human operators to regulate flow by manually adjusting the valve settings.</p></p>
