<p><p>The medical pendant market comprises four main types: ceiling-mounted, wall-mounted, free-standing, and others. Ceiling-mounted pendants are fixed to the ceiling and offer a wide range of movement. Wall-mounted pendants are secured on the wall, providing flexibility in positioning medical equipment. Free-standing pendants are independent structures that can be moved easily. The "others" category includes various types of pendants that may not fit into the aforementioned categories. Each type of medical pendant caters to specific needs, offering different advantages and convenience to healthcare providers.</p></p>
