<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Lithography systems are used in various industries for printing and manufacturing purposes. Contact printers are the oldest and simplest type, where the substrate directly contacts the mask. Proximity printers use a small gap between the mask and the substrate, enabling higher resolution. Projection printers employ lenses to project an image of the mask onto the substrate, offering even greater resolution. These market types cater to different needs and preferences, with contact printers being more cost-effective, proximity printers providing enhanced accuracy, and projection printers delivering the highest resolution.</p></p>
