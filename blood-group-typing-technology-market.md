<p><strong>The Blood Group Typing Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PCR-Based and Microarray Techniques</li><li>Assay-Based Techniques</li><li>Massively Parallel Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blood group typing technology market is divided into three types. Firstly, PCR-based and microarray techniques analyze DNA to determine blood types. Secondly, assay-based techniques use antibodies to identify blood group antigens. Lastly, massively parallel sequencing technology reads the genetic code to determine blood types. PCR-based and microarray techniques are based on DNA analysis, whereas assay-based techniques use antibodies and massively parallel sequencing reads the genetic code. These technologies play a crucial role in blood transfusion, transplantation, and prenatal testing by accurately identifying blood types.</p></p>
