<p><strong>The Industrial Vibration Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Velocity Sensor</li><li>Displacement Sensor</li><li>Acceleration Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Vibration Sensor market is divided into three main types: Velocity Sensor, Displacement Sensor, and Acceleration Sensor. Velocity Sensors measure the rate at which an object's position changes over time, providing information about the amplitude and frequency of vibrations. Displacement Sensors determine the distance between an object's original position and its current position, giving insights into the magnitude of vibrations. Acceleration Sensors measure the rate at which an object's velocity changes over time, offering data about the intensity and direction of vibrations. These sensors are crucial for monitoring and maintaining machine health in various industries.</p></p>
