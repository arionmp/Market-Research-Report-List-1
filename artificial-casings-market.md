<p><p>The Artificial Casings market encompasses various types including Collagen Casing, Cellulose Casing, Fibrous Casing, and Plastic Casing. Collagen Casing, made from animal collagen, has high elasticity, strength, and uniformity, making it ideal for sausages and processed meats. Cellulose Casing, derived from plant cellulose, offers superior strength and stability, enabling seamless cooking and easy peeling. Fibrous Casing, composed of cellulose fibers and paper, provides excellent smoke penetration and moisture retention, suitable for salamis and summer sausages. Plastic Casing, often made from polyamide or polyethylene, ensures high barrier properties and durability, ideal for cooked and smoked sausages.</p></p>
