<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Roll Inspection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Eddy Current Technology</li><li>Ultrasonic Technology</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roll inspection system market types can be broadly categorized into three - Eddy Current Technology, Ultrasonic Technology, and Others. Eddy Current Technology utilizes electromagnetic induction to detect defects like cracks and irregularities in the surface of the roll. Ultrasonic Technology uses high-frequency sound waves to identify flaws by measuring the time taken by sound to reflect back from the roll. The Others category includes various non-destructive testing techniques such as magnetic particle inspection and dye penetrant testing for roll inspection purposes.</p></p>
