<p><p>The Edge Guiding System market can be categorized into four main types: sensor, controller, actuator, and other markets. Sensors in this context are devices that detect and measure the position or movement of the edge being guided. Controllers are responsible for analyzing the sensor data and determining the appropriate corrective actions. Actuators are the components that physically adjust the position of the edge. The "Other" market includes supplementary products or services that support the functioning of the edge guiding system, such as communication interfaces, software, and maintenance services.</p></p>
