<p><p>Ordinary Current Transformers consist of two main market types: Primary Winding and Secondary Winding. The Primary Winding market focuses on manufacturing and selling transformers with primary windings that are intended to be connected to the power source or circuit being monitored. These transformers step down the current so it can be accurately measured by the secondary winding market. The Secondary Winding market specializes in producing transformers with secondary windings that are connected to the measuring or monitoring instruments. This market plays a crucial role in accurately measuring and monitoring electrical currents in various applications.</p></p>
