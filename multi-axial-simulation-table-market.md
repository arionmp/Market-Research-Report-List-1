<p><ul><li>Electric Simulation Table</li><li>Hydraulic Simulation Table</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-axial simulation table market is segmented into two main types: electric simulation tables and hydraulic simulation tables. Electric simulation tables use electric motors to generate motion and provide accurate simulations for testing purposes. They offer precise control and high repeatability. On the other hand, hydraulic simulation tables utilize hydraulic systems to generate motion. They provide high force and large displacement capabilities, making them suitable for testing heavy and large objects. Both types of simulation tables are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and defense for product testing and development.</p></p>
