<p><p>The Online Exam and Assessment Proctoring Software market consists of three types: </p><p>1. Real-Time Live Broadcast: This type allows for live broadcasting of exams or assessments. Students can take tests remotely while being monitored in real-time by proctors. </p><p>2. Automatic Invigilation: This type uses AI technology to automatically invigilate and monitor students during exams. It detects suspicious activities such as cheating and alerts the proctors for intervention. </p><p>3. Record Replay: This type records the entire exam session, including audio, video, and screen activities. Proctors can review the recordings later to ensure integrity and fairness in the exam process.</p></p>
