<p><p>The microscopy market is segmented into two main types: optical microscope and electron microscope. Optical microscopes use visible light to magnify and observe samples, making them suitable for biological and medical research. They are cost-effective and widely used due to their ease of use. On the other hand, electron microscopes use a beam of electrons instead of light, allowing for higher magnification and resolution. They are mainly used for studying non-biological specimens and provide detailed images of the sample's surface and internal structure.</p></p>
