<p><p>Smart bolts are a diverse category of bolts that are embedded with various smart technologies for monitoring and control purposes. Hex bolts have a hexagonal head and are commonly used in construction and machinery. Flange bolts have a wider surface area and are ideal for applications requiring strong joints. Socket bolts have a cylindrical head and are used with a socket wrench for greater torque. Stud bolts lack a head, being threaded on both ends, and are used to join objects together. Other smart bolts encompass unique designs and functionalities catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
