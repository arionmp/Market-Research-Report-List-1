<p><p>Single strip seal expansion joints are commonly used in infrastructure projects to accommodate movement and provide a watertight seal between two adjacent structures. There are two main types of strip seal expansion joints: those with straight heads and those with bending heads. Strip seal expansion joints with straight heads are ideal for linear movements, while those with bending heads are suitable for curved or angular movements. Both types offer durability, flexibility, and effective sealing to prevent water and debris infiltration, ensuring the longevity and functionality of the infrastructure.</p></p>
