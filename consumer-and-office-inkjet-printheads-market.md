<p><strong>The Consumer and Office Inkjet Printheads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Thermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Consumer and Office Inkjet Printheads are divided into two main types: Piezoelectric and Thermal. Piezoelectric printheads use tiny electrically-charged crystals to control ink droplet ejection, creating precise and detailed prints. This technology allows for greater control over ink placement and offers a wider range of colors. On the other hand, Thermal printheads use heat to vaporize the ink, forming tiny bubbles that eject the ink droplets. This method is cost-effective and provides high-speed printing but may result in slightly lower print quality compared to Piezoelectric printheads.</p></p>
