<p><p>FACTS (Flexible AC Transmission Systems) devices are advanced power electronic devices that enhance the efficiency, stability, and capacity of power transmission systems. Two commonly used FACTS devices are SVC (Static Var Compensator) and SVG (Static Var Generator). SVC devices regulate the voltage in electrical grids by controlling the reactive power flow, ensuring a stable power supply. On the other hand, SVG devices inject or absorb reactive power to stabilize the grid during disturbances. Both SVC and SVG technologies play crucial roles in maintaining reliable and efficient power transmission networks.</p></p>
