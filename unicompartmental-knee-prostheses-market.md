<p><strong>The Unicompartmental Knee Prostheses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed-bearing</li><li>Mobile-bearing</li><li>Fixed or Mobile-bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Unicompartmental knee prostheses refer to artificial implants specifically designed to replace only one part of the knee joint. There are three types available in the market: fixed-bearing, mobile-bearing, and fixed or mobile-bearing. Fixed-bearing prostheses consist of a fixed plastic piece between the femur and tibia. Mobile-bearing prostheses allow movement between the plastic component and the metal component. Fixed or mobile-bearing prostheses provide both fixed and mobile options, allowing surgeons to choose the most suitable type based on individual patient needs. These options offer flexibility in knee replacement surgery, catering to different patient requirements and preferences.</p></p>
