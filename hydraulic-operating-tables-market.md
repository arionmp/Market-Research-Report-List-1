<p><p>Hydraulic operating tables are crucial equipment used in surgical procedures to provide optimal positioning and comfort for patients. There are two main types available in the market: electro-hydraulic operating tables and manual-hydraulic operating tables. Electro-hydraulic tables are powered by an electric motor, allowing for convenient height and position adjustments through a control panel. On the other hand, manual-hydraulic tables require manual pumping to adjust the table's height and positioning. Both types play a vital role in ensuring efficiency and precision during surgical procedures, catering to different preferences and needs of medical professionals.</p></p>
