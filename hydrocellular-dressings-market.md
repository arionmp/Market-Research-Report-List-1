<p><p>Hydrocellular dressings are widely used in wound care management. Non-adhesive hydrocellular dressings are designed to be secured with tape or a secondary dressing, providing a moist healing environment for moderate to heavily exuding wounds. These dressings are less likely to stick to the wound bed, facilitating easy removal and minimizing patient discomfort. On the other hand, adhesive hydrocellular dressings have a built-in adhesive border, eliminating the need for tape or secondary dressings. This type of dressing is suitable for wounds with minimal to low exudate levels and offers enhanced convenience during application and removal.</p></p>
