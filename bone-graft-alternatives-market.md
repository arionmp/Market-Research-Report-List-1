<p><ul><li>Allograft</li><li>Xenograft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone graft alternatives market offers different types of grafts, including allografts and xenografts. Allografts refer to tissue grafts taken from a human donor, processed, and then transplanted into another patient. Xenografts, on the other hand, involve using grafts taken from a different animal species and processed for transplantation. These alternatives serve as substitutes for autografts, which are grafts taken from the patient's own body. Allografts and xenografts provide options for patients who may not be suitable candidates for autografts or prefer not to use their own tissue.</p></p>
