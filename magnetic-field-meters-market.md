<p><p>The market for magnetic field meters can be classified into two types: voltage magnetic field meters and contract magnetic field meters. Voltage magnetic field meters are devices used to measure the magnetic field generated by voltage sources such as power lines or transformers. Contract magnetic field meters, on the other hand, are instruments used to measure the magnetic field levels before and after the implementation of a contract or agreement, mainly in industries or construction sites. Both types serve the purpose of assessing and monitoring magnetic field levels for various applications.</p></p>
