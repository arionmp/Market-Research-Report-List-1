<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The magnetic iron separator market includes two main types: permanent magnetic iron separator and electro magnetic iron separator. The permanent magnetic iron separator utilizes permanent magnets to separate iron particles from materials. It is ideal for applications where continuous removal of metallic contaminants is required. On the other hand, the electro magnetic iron separator uses an electromagnetic coil to create a magnetic field, which attracts and separates iron particles. This type of separator is commonly used in industries where the magnetic field needs to be adjusted or turned off periodically.</p></p>
