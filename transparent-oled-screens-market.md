<p><p>Transparent OLED screens are a type of display technology that allows users to see through the screen while also displaying images. AMOLED screens (Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode) are the most advanced type, featuring improved image quality, color reproduction, and deeper black levels. They are commonly used in high-end smartphones and tablets. PMOLED screens (Passive-Matrix OLED) are less advanced and offer simpler features and lower image quality. They are mainly used in low-cost devices like fitness bands and small display applications. Both types of screens contribute to the expanding transparent display market.</p></p>
