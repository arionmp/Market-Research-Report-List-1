<p><strong>The FPC Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Antennas</li><li>External Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FPC antennas are categorized into two main types: internal antennas and external antennas. Internal antennas are embedded within the electronic device, providing a compact design and ease of integration. They are ideal for devices with limited space or aesthetic considerations. On the other hand, external antennas are mounted on the exterior of the device. These antennas offer flexibility in terms of orientation and placement, allowing for improved signal reception and range. Both types of FPC antennas serve the purpose of transmitting and receiving wireless signals but differ in their physical location.</p></p>
