<p><p>Custom T-shirt printing market consists of various types, including silk screen printing, digital printing, and plot printing. Silk screen printing involves pressing ink through a mesh screen onto the fabric, creating a long-lasting and vibrant design. Digital printing utilizes inkjet technology to print designs directly onto the fabric, offering high-quality and detailed prints. Plot printing employs a plotter machine that cuts the design from a vinyl sheet and heat presses it onto the fabric, resulting in durable and customizable prints. These printing methods cater to different customer preferences and requirements in the custom T-shirt market.</p></p>
