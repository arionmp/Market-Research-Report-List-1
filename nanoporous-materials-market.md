<p><p>Nanoporous materials are widely used in various industries due to their unique properties. Zeolites, clays, activated carbon, silica gel, activated alumina, and other materials are commonly used in the market. Zeolites are crystalline materials with uniform pores that are used for adsorption and separation processes. Clays, such as montmorillonite and bentonite, have layered structures and are used for catalysis and drug delivery applications. Activated carbon has a large surface area and is used for gas purification and water treatment. Silica gel is a highly porous material used for moisture control. Activated alumina is used for drying gases and removing impurities. Other nanoporous materials offer various functionalities in different applications.</p></p>
