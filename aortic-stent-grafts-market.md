The market for aortic stent grafts can be categorized into three types based on their intended use. Endovascular Aneurysm Repair (EVAR) involves placing a stent graft within the diseased segment of the aorta to exclude the aneurysm from blood circulation. Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm Repair specifically focuses on treating aneurysms located in the abdominal aorta. Lastly, the "other" market segment includes stent grafts used for various other conditions and locations within the aorta.</p></p>
