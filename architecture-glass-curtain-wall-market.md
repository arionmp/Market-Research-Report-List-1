<p><ul><li>Frame Support Curtain Wall</li><li>All-glass Curtain Wall</li><li>Point Support Curtain Wall</li><li>Unit-typed Curtain Wall</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Architecture Glass Curtain Wall Market is divided into several types. Frame Support Curtain Wall refers to a system where the curtain wall is supported by a metal frame structure. All-glass Curtain Wall is a system that uses glass panels without any visible frames or supports. Point Support Curtain Wall is supported by discrete points along the glass panels, providing a sleek and transparent appearance. Unit-typed Curtain Wall involves preassembled units that are installed onto the building's structure, offering a faster and more efficient installation process. These types cater to different aesthetic and functional requirements in the construction industry.</p></p>
