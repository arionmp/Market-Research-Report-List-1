<p><p>The infertility treatment devices and equipment market includes various types of devices used in procedures to treat infertility. Ovum aspiration pumps are used to retrieve eggs from the ovaries. Sperm separation devices help in separating healthy sperm for fertilization. Sperm analyzer systems assess the quality of sperm. Micromanipulator systems are used in procedures like intracytoplasmic sperm injection. Incubators provide a controlled environment for embryo development. Cryosystems are used for the freezing and storage of embryos. Imaging systems and microscopes aid in visualizing eggs, sperm, and embryos. Other devices may include catheters, fertility monitors, and labware used in assisted reproductive techniques.</p></p>
