<p><ul><li>Rotary Bits</li><li>Fixed Cutter Bits</li><li>Roller Cone Bits</li><li>DTH Hammers Bits</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mining drill bits market can be categorized into different types based on their design and functionality. Rotary bits are commonly used and have a rotating mechanism to drill through rocks. Fixed cutter bits have non-rotating blades and are suitable for drilling hard formations. Roller cone bits have cone-shaped rolling cutters for efficient drilling in various formations. DTH Hammers bits use a percussion mechanism to break rocks and are often used in deep drilling. Lastly, "others" include specialized drill bits such as diamond bits and reamer bits that are used for specific drilling purposes.</p></p>
