<p><ul><li>Benchtop RTES</li><li>Portable RTES</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radar Target Simulator (RTES) market can be categorized into two types: Benchtop RTES and Portable RTES. Benchtop RTES refers to the radar target simulators that are designed for stationary use in laboratories or testing facilities. They are larger in size and offer advanced functionalities for complex radar testing and evaluation. On the other hand, Portable RTES are compact and lightweight simulators, which are easily transportable to be used in the field or any remote location. They are suitable for on-site radar testing and training purposes, offering convenient mobility and ease of deployment.</p></p>
