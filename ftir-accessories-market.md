<p><p>FTIR accessories market types refer to the various products available for Fourier Transform Infrared (FTIR) spectroscopy. Transmission sampling products allow infrared radiation to pass through samples for analysis. Attenuated Total Reflection (ATR) products measure the infrared absorption of samples placed on a crystal surface. Diffuse reflectance products analyze the scattering of infrared radiation from samples. Specular reflectance products study the reflection of infrared radiation from smooth surfaces. Other accessories include gas cells, liquid cells, and sample holders for specific sample types. These products enable researchers to obtain accurate and reliable spectroscopic data using FTIR techniques.</p></p>
