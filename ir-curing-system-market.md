<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The IR (infrared) curing system market consists of three types: shortwave, mediumwave, and longwave. Shortwave systems emit high-intensity radiation with a wavelength range of 0.8 to 1.4 microns, making them ideal for quick and efficient curing of heat-sensitive materials. Mediumwave systems emit radiation with a wavelength range of 1.4 to 3 microns, suitable for general-purpose curing. Longwave systems emit low-intensity radiation with a wavelength range of 3 to 10 microns, making them suitable for deep penetration curing of thick coatings and substrates. These types cater to different curing requirements in various industrial applications.</p></p>
