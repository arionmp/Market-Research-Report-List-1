<p><p>Intelligent heat detectors are devices used to detect the presence of increasing temperature or fire in a certain area. Fixed temperature detectors are set to trigger an alarm when the temperature reaches a specific threshold, while differential temperature detectors work by comparing the rate of temperature rise in different parts of the device. Fixed temperature detectors are more suitable for environments with consistent temperature levels, while differential temperature detectors are preferred in areas where temperature changes are more rapid, such as commercial kitchens or data centers.</p></p>
