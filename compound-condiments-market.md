<p><p>The compound condiments market comprises three main types: solid condiments, liquid condiments, and compound sauces. Solid condiments refer to products such as spices, dry rubs, and seasoning mixes that are in solid form and added to food for flavor enhancement. Liquid condiments, on the other hand, include sauces like ketchup, mustard, and soy sauce, which are commonly used for dipping or as a topping. Compound sauces are a combination of multiple ingredients, like mayonnaise or barbecue sauce, designed to provide a versatile flavor profile to various dishes.</p></p>
