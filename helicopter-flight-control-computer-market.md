<p><p>The Helicopter Flight Control Computer (FCC) market can be classified into two main types: OEM and Aftermarket. The OEM market refers to the sale of FCCs directly from the original equipment manufacturer to helicopter manufacturers during the production stage. On the other hand, the Aftermarket market deals with the sale of FCCs to helicopter operators or maintenance and repair organizations after the helicopters are already in service. In the Aftermarket, FCCs may be replaced due to wear and tear or upgraded to enhance helicopter performance, safety, or compliance with new regulations.</p></p>
