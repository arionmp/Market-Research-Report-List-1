Carbon graphite vanes are made purely from carbon and offer good resistance to wear and heat. Metal impregnated carbon graphite vanes have metal impregnation in their structure, providing enhanced durability and conductivity. Resin bonded graphite vanes consist of graphite particles mixed with resin, optimizing their strength and stability. Each type caters to different needs in vacuum pump applications, offering varying levels of performance and reliability.</p></p>
