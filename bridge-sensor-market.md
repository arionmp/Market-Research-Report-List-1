<p><p>The Bridge Sensor market includes various types of sensors used for measuring physical quantities. Quarter-bridge sensors use one resistive strain gauge and can measure only one force component. Half-bridge sensors have two resistive strain gauges, allowing measurement of both axial and bending forces. Full-bridge sensors utilize four resistive strain gauges and can measure all force components. Other types of bridge sensors may include dual-bridge, double-ended shear beam, or S-type bridge sensors. Each type of sensor offers different capabilities and is suitable for specific applications based on accuracy requirements and force measurement needs.</p></p>
