<p><p>The low-voltage electric motor market consists of different types of motors, including synchronous motors and induction motors. Synchronous motors operate at a fixed speed by synchronizing with the frequency of the electrical supply. They offer high efficiency and are commonly used in applications that require precise control, such as robotics and industrial machinery. On the other hand, induction motors operate at variable speeds and are widely used in residential and commercial applications due to their simplicity, reliability, and cost-effectiveness. These motors are commonly found in appliances, HVAC systems, and pumps.</p></p>
