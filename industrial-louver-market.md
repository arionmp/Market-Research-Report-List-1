<p><p>The industrial louver market consists of two main types: fixed blade and adjustable blade. Fixed blade louvers have stationary blades that are set at a fixed angle and cannot be adjusted. These louvers provide a permanent airflow direction and are commonly used in applications where a constant airflow is required. On the other hand, adjustable blade louvers have movable blades that can be adjusted to control the airflow direction. These louvers offer flexibility in directing airflow and are often utilized in applications where variable airflow is desired.</p></p>
