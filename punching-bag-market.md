<p><p>The punching bag market comprises different types of bags that serve specific purposes. Heavy bags are large and durable bags intended for power training and overall conditioning. Speed bags are small and fast-moving, designed to enhance hand-eye coordination and develop speed. Uppercut bags are shaped like a U and are used to practice uppercut punches. Double-ended bags are attached to the floor and ceiling, providing unpredictable movement to improve accuracy and reflexes. These various bag types cater to different training needs and preferences within the boxing and martial arts communities.</p></p>
