<p><p>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) Sensor market includes two main types: Torque Only Sensor (TOS) and Torque Angle Sensor (TAS). TOS measures and transmits the torque applied by the driver to the steering column. It is designed to provide accurate torque information for steering assistance. On the other hand, TAS measures the torque angle, which is the amount the steering wheel has turned. This information helps in determining the steering angle and provides feedback to the driver, enhancing the overall steering experience.</p></p>
