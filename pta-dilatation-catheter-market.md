It is a minimally invasive procedure that involves using a catheter with an inflatable balloon at its tip to widen and unblock the narrowed or blocked blood vessels.</p><p>The market for PTA Dilatation Catheter is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. One of the key trends is the development of drug-coated PTA dilatation catheters.
