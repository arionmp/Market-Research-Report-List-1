<p><p>Metalworking machines refer to machines used in the process of shaping or machining metal materials. There are various types of metalworking machines available in the market. </p><p>Single/multistation transfer machines are versatile machines that can perform different operations on a workpiece, reducing the need for manual intervention. </p><p>Lathes for removing metal are machines used to rotate a workpiece rapidly while a cutting tool is applied to remove material and create a desired shape. </p><p>Metal drilling machines are used to create holes in metal using drill bits. </p><p>Other metalworking machines include milling machines, grinding machines, and bending machines, among others. These machines help in shaping, cutting, drilling, and other metal fabrication processes.</p></p>
