<p><p>Labeling software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based labeling software is accessed and operated over the internet, eliminating the need for physical infrastructure on the user's end. It offers the advantage of scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premise labeling software is installed and operated on the user's own servers and infrastructure, providing full control and customization. This type of software is preferred by organizations with strict security requirements or limited internet connectivity.</p></p>
