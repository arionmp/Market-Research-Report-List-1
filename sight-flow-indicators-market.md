<p><p>Sight flow indicators are devices used to visually monitor the flow of liquids or gases in a system. The market offers different types of indicators based on their design and functionality. Rotary indicators comprise a rotating wheel or paddle, which moves with the flow. Flapper indicators use a swinging flapper to indicate flow. Flutter indicators have a vane that moves in response to flow. Ball indicators use a floating ball to provide visual confirmation. Drip indicators have a drip chamber to monitor slow flow rates. These indicators cater to various applications in industries like oil and gas, chemical, and water treatment.</p></p>
