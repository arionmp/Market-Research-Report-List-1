<p><p>The electrical machinery market can be categorized into two main types: AC type and DC type. AC type machines are designed to run on alternating current, which means the direction of current changes periodically. These machines are commonly used in most household appliances and electric power distribution systems. On the other hand, DC type machines operate on direct current, with a constant flow of current in one direction. These machines are usually found in batteries, electric vehicles, and certain industrial applications. Both AC and DC machines play crucial roles in powering various electrical devices and systems.</p></p>
