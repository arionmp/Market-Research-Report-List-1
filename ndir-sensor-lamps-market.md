<p><p>NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) sensor lamps are a type of optical gas measurement technology used for the detection of gases such as carbon dioxide, methane, and other hydrocarbons. These lamps emit infrared light, which passes through a measurement chamber containing the gas to be analyzed. The absorption of infrared light by the gas molecules is measured, and the concentration of the gas is determined based on the intensity of absorption.</p><p>The NDIR sensor lamps market is witnessing significant growth due to several factors. The increased need for accurate and reliable gas detection and monitoring systems in industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and manufacturing is driving the demand for NDIR sensor lamps. Stringent environmental regulations regarding gas emissions are also contributing to market growth, as these lamps are highly effective in identifying and quantifying harmful gases.</p><p>Additionally, the rising adoption of NDIR sensors in the automotive sector for monitoring vehicle exhaust gases is further fueling market growth.
