<p><p>The Oil Free Scroll Compressor Market consists of two main types: Fixed Scroll and Orbiting Scroll. In Fixed Scroll compressors, one scroll is stationary while the other moves in a circular motion, creating compression. This design is simpler and requires less maintenance. On the other hand, Orbiting Scroll compressors have both scrolls moving in an orbital motion, resulting in higher efficiency and improved performance. The Orbiting Scroll design also offers better cooling capabilities and reduces noise levels. Both types cater to various industrial applications, each offering distinct advantages for specific needs.</p></p>
