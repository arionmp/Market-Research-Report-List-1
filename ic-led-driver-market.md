<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>IC LED Driver market can be categorized into two types: Constant Current and Regulated. Constant Current drivers are designed to provide a consistent output current to the LED, ensuring stable illumination regardless of voltage fluctuations. On the other hand, Regulated drivers maintain a stable voltage to the LED, compensating for fluctuations in the LED forward voltage to maintain a constant brightness. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring reliable and efficient performance of LED lighting systems in various applications, such as residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
