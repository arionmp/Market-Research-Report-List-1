<p><p>Automotive cloud based solutions market comprises of two main types: private cloud and public cloud. Private cloud involves hosting services on a dedicated secure network, providing exclusive access to a single organization. Public cloud, on the other hand, offers services to multiple users over a shared network infrastructure. Private cloud ensures privacy and control over data, while public cloud offers scalability and cost efficiency. Both types play a significant role in driving innovation and optimization in the automotive industry.</p></p>
