<p><p>Breast reconstruction is a process that aims to restore the shape, size, and appearance of a breast after a mastectomy or lumpectomy. Different types of breast reconstruction methods are available. Inframammary reconstruction involves placing implants or creating a tissue flap through an incision made underneath the breast fold. Peri-areolar reconstruction involves making an incision around the areola and placing an implant or using tissue flaps. Trans-axillary reconstruction utilizes an incision in the armpit to insert implants or create a tissue flap. Transumbilical reconstruction involves making an incision in the belly button area to insert implants.</p></p>
