<p><ul><li>Single Phase Hammer Crusher</li><li>Double Phase Hammer Crusher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hammer Crusher for Mining Market types can be categorized into Single Phase Hammer Crusher and Double Phase Hammer Crusher. A Single Phase Hammer Crusher operates with a single rotor, spinning at high speed, which impacts materials against the chamber wall and crusher blowbars. This type is suitable for crushing medium-hard to hard materials. In contrast, a Double Phase Hammer Crusher utilizes two rotors, each with separate motor drives, providing higher crushing capacity and finer product size. This type is ideal for crushing softer materials and achieving a more uniform particle size distribution.</p></p>
