<p><strong>The Electrostatic Chucks (ESCs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Force Type</li><li>Johnson-Rahbeck Force Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrostatic Chucks (ESCs) are used in semiconductor manufacturing to secure wafers during the fabrication process. Coulomb Force Type ESCs rely on Coulomb's Law to create an electrostatic force between the chuck and the wafer, ensuring adhesion. On the other hand, Johnson-Rahbeck Force Type ESCs utilize the Johnson-Rahbeck effect, utilizing a voltage gradient to impose an electrostatic force. Both types ensure wafer stability, but Coulomb Force ESCs are simpler, while Johnson-Rahbeck Force ESCs are more precise and versatile.</p></p>
