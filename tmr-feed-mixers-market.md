<p><p>The TMR (Total Mixed Ration) feed mixers market offers two main types of mixers: self-propelled and stationary. Self-propelled mixers are vehicles equipped with a mixing unit and are capable of maneuvering and blending feed ingredients directly on the farm. On the other hand, stationary mixers remain fixed at a given location, allowing farmers to bring the feed ingredients to the mixer for blending. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific needs of the farmer, with self-propelled mixers offering convenience and mobility, while stationary mixers provide ease of loading feed ingredients.</p></p>
