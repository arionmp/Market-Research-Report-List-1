<p><p>The beverage centrifugal pump market consists of two main types: single-stage pump and multi-stage pump. A single-stage pump is designed to work efficiently in applications with low pressure requirements. It has a simple structure and is cost-effective. On the other hand, a multi-stage pump is suitable for high-pressure applications. It consists of multiple impellers that work together to generate high pressure. Multi-stage pumps are commonly used in industries where high pressure is necessary, such as beverage production and processing plants.</p></p>
