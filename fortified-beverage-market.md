Consumers are increasingly demanding beverages that are free from artificial ingredients, preservatives, and additives. Additionally, manufacturers are focusing on incorporating novel ingredients and flavors to differentiate their products and meet evolving consumer demands. Personalization and customization of fortified beverages are also gaining traction, with companies offering options to tailor the nutrient content according to individual needs.</p><p>Overall, the fortified beverage market is poised for significant growth in the coming years, fueled by increasing consumer awareness, changing dietary preferences, and advancements in beverage formulation. As more consumers seek convenient ways to maintain a healthy lifestyle, the demand for fortified beverages is likely to surge, propelling market growth in the foreseeable future.</p></p>
