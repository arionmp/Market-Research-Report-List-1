<p><p>Forestry tractors are classified into two main types: four-wheel drive and two-wheel drive. Four-wheel drive tractors are equipped with power to all four wheels, providing better traction and weight distribution for optimal performance in challenging terrains. These tractors are suitable for heavy-duty operations and are commonly used in logging and forestry activities. On the other hand, two-wheel drive tractors are designed for lighter tasks and are more suitable for flat or less demanding terrains. They have power only in two wheels and are commonly used for smaller-scale forestry operations.</p></p>
