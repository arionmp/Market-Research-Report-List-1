<p><p>Bipolar Electrostatic Chucks are a type of chuck used in the semiconductor industry to hold and secure wafers during manufacturing processes. The market for these chucks can be classified into two types: Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chuck and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chuck. Coulomb Type chucks use the principle of electrostatic attraction and repulsion to hold the wafers in place. On the other hand, JR Type chucks use the combined force of Coulomb electrostatics and magnetism. Both types are crucial for efficient and precise wafer handling in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
