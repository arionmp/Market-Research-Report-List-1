<p><p>A high acuity ventilator, also known as a critical care ventilator, is a medical device used to provide breathing support for patients with severe respiratory conditions. High acuity ventilators are commonly used in intensive care units (ICUs), emergency rooms, and other critical care settings.</p><p>The high acuity ventilator market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding in the forecast period. The market growth is primarily driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic respiratory diseases, such as chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) and asthma, which require long-term ventilator support. <p><ul><li>Non-invasive Ventilator</li><li>Invasive Ventilator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high acuity ventilator market comprises two main types: non-invasive ventilators and invasive ventilators. Non-invasive ventilators are designed to support breathing without the need for inserting a tube into the patient's airway. They are used for patients with milder respiratory conditions or for short-term usage. In contrast, invasive ventilators involve the insertion of a tube into the patient's airway to deliver breaths directly. These are used for patients with more severe respiratory issues or for longer durations. Both types play a crucial role in providing respiratory support to patients with high acuity needs.</p></p>
