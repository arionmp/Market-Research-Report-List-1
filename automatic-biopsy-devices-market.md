<p><strong>The Automatic Biopsy Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-Automatic Biopsy Device</li><li>Fully Automatic Biopsy Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic biopsy devices market encompasses two main types: semi-automatic biopsy devices and fully automatic biopsy devices. Semi-automatic biopsy devices require partial manual assistance from the operator during the biopsy procedure. They typically combine automatic sample acquisition with manual sample placement. On the other hand, fully automatic biopsy devices perform the entire biopsy process automatically, including sample acquisition and placement. These devices eliminate the need for manual intervention, enabling a more efficient and reliable biopsy procedure.</p></p>
