<p><ul><li>FPC Connector</li><li>Board to Board Connector</li><li>I/O Connector</li><li>Card Connector</li><li>Power Connector</li><li>RF Connector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cell phone connectors market consists of various types of connectors used in cell phones. FPC connectors, or Flexible Printed Circuit connectors, are used to connect flexible circuits inside the phone. Board to Board connectors are used for interconnecting different circuit boards within the phone. I/O connectors enable input and output connections for devices such as headphones and charging cables. Card connectors allow the insertion of memory cards or SIM cards. Power connectors enable the connection of the phone to power sources. RF connectors are used for wireless communication between the phone and antennas.</p></p>
