<p><p>Potentiometer titrators are used in analytical chemistry to determine the concentration of a solution by measuring the potential difference. There are two main types of potentiometric titrators on the market - fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic titrators perform all the steps of the titration process automatically, including reagent addition and endpoint detection. Semi-automatic titrators require manual initiation of reagent addition and endpoint detection, but still provide automated calculations and data logging. Both types offer accurate and efficient analysis, but fully automatic titrators save more time and labor.</p></p>
