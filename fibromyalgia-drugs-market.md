<p><ul><li>Serotonin Norepinephrine Reuptake Inhibitors</li><li>GABA Analogs</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fibromyalgia Drugs market primarily consists of three types: Serotonin Norepinephrine Reuptake Inhibitors (SNRIs), GABA Analogs, and Others. SNRIs work by increasing the levels of serotonin and norepinephrine in the brain, helping to alleviate pain and improve mood. GABA Analogs work by targeting gamma-aminobutyric acid (GABA) receptors, which are implicated in pain regulation. The "Other" category includes various drugs such as muscle relaxants, anti-inflammatory drugs, and opioids, which can be prescribed to manage fibromyalgia symptoms. These drugs aim to provide relief from pain, fatigue, and sleep disturbances commonly experienced by fibromyalgia patients.</p></p>
