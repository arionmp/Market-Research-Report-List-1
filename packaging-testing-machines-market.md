<p><p>Packaging testing machines are used to evaluate the strength and durability of packaging materials and products. Tensile strength testing equipment measures the maximum force a material can withstand when stretched or pulled. Compression testing equipment evaluates a package's ability to withstand compressive forces. Drop testing equipment assesses the impact resistance of packaging by dropping it from a certain height. Other types of packaging testing machines may include friction testing equipment and vibration testing equipment, which examine factors like sliding resistance and mechanical stress on packaged goods.</p></p>
