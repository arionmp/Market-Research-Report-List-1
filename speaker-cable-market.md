<p><p>The speaker cable market consists of two main types: single mode cable and multimode cable. Single mode cable is designed to transmit light signals over long distances with minimal interference. It is commonly used in telecommunications applications. In contrast, multimode cable can transmit multiple light signals simultaneously, but over shorter distances. It is commonly used for short-range communication in local area networks (LANs) and data centers. Both types cater to different needs, with single mode cable excelling in long-distance transmission and multimode cable being suitable for shorter distances.</p></p>
