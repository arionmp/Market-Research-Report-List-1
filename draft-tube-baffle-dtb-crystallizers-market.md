<p><p>Draft Tube Baffle (DTB) crystallizers are used in multiple industries for the crystallization of various materials. There are two main types of DTB crystallizers - vertical and horizontal. </p><p>Vertical DTB crystallizers consist of a vertical tube where the supersaturated solution is introduced at the top and flows downward. The interior baffles enhance mixing and promote nucleation and crystal growth. This design is suitable for applications with high heat transfer requirements.</p><p>Horizontal DTB crystallizers have a horizontal cylindrical vessel with baffles that create a flow path. The solution is introduced at one end and flows through the baffles, allowing for maximum contact with the cooling or heating medium. This configuration is often preferred for processes that require longer residence time or have low heat transfer requirements.</p></p>
