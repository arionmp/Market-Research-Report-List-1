<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Boiler Waste Heat Recovery System market comprises two main types: Waterwall Hrsg and Cross Flow Two-Drum Hrsg. The Waterwall Hrsg system utilizes a series of tubes where hot flue gases from the boiler pass, transferring heat to water inside the tubes. On the other hand, the Cross Flow Two-Drum Hrsg system has two drums, one for steam and the other for water, facilitating efficient heat transfer. Both systems enable the recovery of waste heat from boilers, improving overall energy efficiency and reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
