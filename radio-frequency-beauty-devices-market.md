<p><p>The radio frequency beauty devices market includes various types of devices: Monopolar, Bipolar, and Multiple Polar. Monopolar devices utilize a single electrode to deliver radio frequency energy, which penetrates deep into the skin to stimulate collagen production. Bipolar devices have two electrodes and the radio frequency energy is focused between them, providing targeted heating to specific areas of the skin. Multiple Polar devices have multiple electrodes that offer a combination of both shallow and deep penetration to effectively tighten and rejuvenate the skin.</p></p>
