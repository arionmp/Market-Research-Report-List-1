<p><p>Public cloud services refer to the provision of computing resources, such as storage, servers, databases, software, and applications, over the internet by a third-party service provider. The popularity of public cloud services is increasing rapidly due to their scalability, cost-efficiency, and flexibility.</p><p>The public cloud service market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 12.5% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to factors such as the increasing adoption of cloud services by organizations to streamline their operations, the rising demand for data storage and backup solutions, and the shift towards digital transformation.</p><p>Moreover, the market growth can be attributed to the emergence of new trends and advancements in public cloud services. One of the latest trends in the market is the adoption of multi-cloud strategies, where organizations use multiple cloud service providers to leverage the unique features and capabilities of each provider. This approach allows organizations to reduce vendor lock-in, enhance flexibility, and optimize their cloud resources.</p><p>Another trend in the public cloud service market is the increasing adoption of serverless computing. This model enables developers to focus on writing code without the need to manage servers or infrastructure. Serverless computing offers improved scalability, reduced costs, and faster time-to-market, driving its adoption in various industries.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing a rise in the demand for artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) capabilities within public cloud services. <p><p>The Public Cloud Service Market offers various types of services. Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) provides virtualized computing resources, like servers and storage, via the cloud. Cloud Bursting allows organizations to augment their existing resources with additional cloud resources during peak demand. Multi Tenant Computing enables multiple users to share the same computing infrastructure, maximizing resource utilization. Resource pooling involves aggregating resources like storage and memory to serve multiple clients efficiently. Platform as a Service (PaaS) provides a platform for developers to build and deploy applications without managing the underlying infrastructure. Software as a Service (SaaS) delivers software applications over the internet, eliminating the need for local installations.</p></p>
