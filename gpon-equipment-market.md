<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The GPON Equipment market includes Optical Line Terminal (OLT), Optical Network Terminal (ONT), and Passive Optical Splitters. An Optical Line Terminal (OLT) acts as the central communication point in a GPON network. It connects to various ONTs which serve as end-user devices. ONTs enable users to access services like internet, voice, and video. Lastly, Passive Optical Splitters divide the optical signal among different ONTs. Together, these components form a GPON network, delivering high-speed internet and other services to end-users efficiently.</p></p>
