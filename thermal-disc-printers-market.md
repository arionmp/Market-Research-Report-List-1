<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Thermal disc printers are categorized into different market types based on the printing technology they use. Direct thermal printers utilize heat-sensitive paper to produce images and do not require ink or toner. Thermal retransfer printers employ a two-step process that transfers the image onto a retransfer film before applying it to the disc surface. Dye-sublimation printers use heat to transfer dye onto a special ribbon, which is then fused onto the disc. Other types of thermal disc printers include those that use heat to etch the image onto the disc surface or use laser technology for printing.</p></p>
