Milk formula is typically made from cow's milk and fortified with essential nutrients for infant growth and development. A2 infant formulas are produced from milk containing only the A2 beta-casein protein, which some believe is easier to digest. Infant complementary foods refer to solid foods introduced to infants alongside breast milk or formula to provide additional nutrition as they grow.</p></p>
