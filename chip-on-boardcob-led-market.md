<p><strong>The Chip-on-board(CoB) LED Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip</li><li>Vertical Chip</li><li>Flip Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip-on-board (CoB) LED market types include lateral chip, vertical chip, and flip chip designs. Lateral chip LEDs have the semiconductor chip located parallel to the substrate, allowing for easy heat dissipation. Vertical chip LEDs have the chip mounted perpendicularly to the substrate, offering better light output and efficiency. Flip chip LEDs have the chip flipped upside down and directly bonded to the substrate, providing improved thermal performance and brightness. Each design has its own advantages and is used in various applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
