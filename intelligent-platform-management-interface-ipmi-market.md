<p><p>Intelligent Platform Management Interface (IPMI) is a specification that defines a set of interfaces, protocols, and platform requirements for managing and monitoring computer systems and their hardware components. It enables out-of-band management, meaning that it allows administrators to manage and control a server or other computing device remotely, even if the device is powered off or unresponsive.</p><p>The IPMI standard includes features such as power control, hardware monitoring, logging and alerting, remote access, and firmware updates. It provides an interface between the hardware management controller (BMC) and the system management software, allowing administrators to monitor and control system components such as processors, memory, storage, and networking devices.</p><p>The IPMI market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Factors such as the increasing demand for efficient server management solutions, rising adoption of cloud computing and virtualization technologies, and the need for remote monitoring and maintenance of IT infrastructure are driving the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the growing trend of data center consolidation and the increasing complexity of managing and controlling large-scale server deployments are further fueling the demand for IPMI solutions. Additionally, the emergence of advanced technologies such as IoT and AI is expected to create new opportunities for IPMI vendors.</p><p>In terms of trends, vendors in the IPMI market are focusing on developing enhanced security features to protect against potential cybersecurity threats. The increasing need for efficient management and maintenance of IT infrastructure, coupled with the advancements in technology, is expected to drive the adoption of IPMI solutions across various industries.</p></p>
