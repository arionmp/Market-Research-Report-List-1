Sit-On DPVs are ridden by the diver, providing a hands-free experience for navigation. Tow-Behind DPVs are attached to the diver, pulling them along underwater. Manned Torpedoes are small submersibles that carry a diver within a protective casing. Other types of DPVs include underwater scooters and propulsion systems that can be handheld or mounted to equipment. Each type offers unique advantages and is used based on the specific requirements of the dive.</p></p>
