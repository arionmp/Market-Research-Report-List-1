<p><p>The military drone/unmanned aerial vehicle market includes three main types: fixed-wing drones, rotary blade drones, and hybrid drones. Fixed-wing drones have a longer flight time and greater range, making them ideal for surveillance and reconnaissance missions. Rotary blade drones, or quadcopters, offer more maneuverability and stability, suitable for close-range operations. Hybrid drones combine features of both fixed-wing and rotary blade drones, providing versatility in different types of missions. Each type of drone serves specific purposes in military operations, contributing to overall mission success.</p></p>
