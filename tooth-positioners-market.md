<p><strong>The Tooth Positioners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-extraction Positioners</li><li>Extraction Positioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tooth positioners are used in orthodontic treatments to correct the alignment of teeth. Non-extraction positioners are a type of tooth positioner that aims to align teeth without the need for tooth extraction. They employ various methods such as wire systems or invisible aligners to gradually move teeth into their proper positions. On the other hand, extraction positioners are a different type of tooth positioner that involves the removal of specific teeth to create space for other teeth to realign. Both non-extraction and extraction positioners play a vital role in orthodontic treatments.</p></p>
