<p><p>The Low Temperature Sensor market can be categorized into different types. The first type is Negative Temperature Coefficient (NTC) sensors, which provide a decrease in resistance as temperature increases. The second type is Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTD), which measure temperature by calculating the change in electrical resistance. Thermocouple Sensors generate a voltage proportional to temperature. Lastly, the market also includes other types of sensors that measure low temperatures. These sensors play a crucial role in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and healthcare by ensuring accurate temperature monitoring and control.</p></p>
