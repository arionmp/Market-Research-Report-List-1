<p><strong>The Restaurant Catering Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Restaurant catering software market types can be classified into two main categories: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based catering software utilizes remote servers to store and process data, offering users the advantage of easy accessibility from any location with an internet connection. It requires no hardware installation or maintenance, providing cost-effectiveness and scalability. On the other hand, on-premises catering software is installed and managed within the restaurant's local infrastructure. It offers greater control and security but requires dedicated hardware, maintenance, and potential upfront costs.</p></p>
