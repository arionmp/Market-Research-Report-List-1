<p><p>Flexible Photovoltaic (PV) cell market consists of various types such as Copper Indium Gallium Selenide (CIGS), Amorphous Silicon (a-Si), Organic Photovoltaics (OPV), and Others. CIGS cells are made from semiconductors that offer high efficiency and cost-effectiveness. a-Si cells are known for their stability, uniformity, and flexible nature, albeit with lower efficiency. OPV cells are made from organic materials, allowing for lightweight, flexible, and low-cost manufacturing options. Other types include dye-sensitized, perovskite, and quantum dot cells. The market for flexible PV cells seeks to provide sustainable and energy-efficient solutions for diverse applications.</p></p>
