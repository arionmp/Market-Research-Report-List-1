<p><p>The Particulate Matter Sensor Market can be divided into three main types: PM2.5, PM10, and Others. PM2.5 sensors are designed to detect particles with a diameter of 2.5 micrometers or less, which are considered highly hazardous to human health. PM10 sensors, on the other hand, are capable of detecting particles with a diameter of 10 micrometers or less. The category labeled as Others encompasses sensors that detect particles with diameters greater than 10 micrometers or employ alternative measurement techniques. These different types cater to the varying needs and requirements of air quality monitoring systems.</p></p>
