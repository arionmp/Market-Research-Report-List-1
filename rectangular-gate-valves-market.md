<p><p>Rectangular gate valves are mechanical devices designed to regulate the flow of fluids through a pipeline system. These valves feature a rectangular orifice that is controlled by a gate, which can be raised or lowered to open or close the valve. This design ensures a full straight-through flow path with minimal turbulence, making rectangular gate valves ideal for applications requiring a low-pressure drop and tight shut-off.</p><p>The market for rectangular gate valves is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for these valves can be attributed to their wide range of applications across industries such as oil and gas, water and wastewater, power generation, chemicals, and others. The growth of these industries, particularly in emerging economies, is driving the market for rectangular gate valves.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising focus on efficient fluid handling systems and the need to meet stringent environmental regulations are further fueling the demand for rectangular gate valves.
