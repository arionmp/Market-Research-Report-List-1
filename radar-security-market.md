<p><p>Ground Surveillance Radar (GSR) Systems refer to radar technology used for monitoring and protecting land-based areas by detecting and tracking objects on the ground in real-time. Air Surveillance Radar (ASR) Systems, on the other hand, focus on monitoring aircraft movements and ensuring airspace safety. Marine Surveillance Radar (MSR) Systems are designed to monitor and secure maritime environments by detecting and tracking vessels and other objects at sea. These radar security systems play a crucial role in enhancing situational awareness and providing early warning capabilities in their respective domains.</p></p>
