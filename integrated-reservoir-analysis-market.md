In contrast, Unconventional reservoirs include tight gas sands, shale formations, and coal beds that require advanced technologies like hydraulic fracturing. Both markets involve analyzing geological data, fluid flow patterns, and reservoir behavior to optimize production and make informed decisions for exploration and development purposes.</p></p>
