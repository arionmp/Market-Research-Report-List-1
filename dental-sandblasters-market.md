<p><strong>The Dental Sandblasters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Automatic</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental sandblasters refer to devices used in dental labs to remove contaminants and debris from dental appliances. There are three types of dental sandblasters in the market. Pneumatic sandblasters work by using compressed air to create a stream of abrasive particles. Automatic sandblasters are equipped with programmable settings for more precise and consistent use. Manual sandblasters rely on manual control of abrasive flow. These different types cater to varying user preferences and the level of precision required in the dental industry.</p></p>
