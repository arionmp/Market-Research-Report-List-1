<p><p>Warehouse Management Software (WMS) is available in two main market types, namely cloud-based (SaaS) and on-premise. Cloud-based WMS is a software-as-a-service solution that operates on remote servers and is accessible through the internet. It offers scalability, remote access, automatic updates, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premise WMS is installed and operated locally on a company's infrastructure. It provides more control, customization options, and data security, but requires higher upfront costs and maintenance responsibilities. Both options cater to different business needs and preferences in managing warehouse operations.</p></p>
