<p><p>Industrial rectifiers are electrical devices used to convert alternating current (AC) into direct current (DC) for industrial applications. There are three main types of rectifiers in the market: LCR, MCR, and HCR. LCR (Low Controlled Rectifiers) are suitable for low-power applications, while MCR (Medium Controlled Rectifiers) are used for medium-power applications. HCR (High Controlled Rectifiers) are designed for high-power and heavy-duty industrial applications. These rectifiers offer efficient and reliable conversion of AC to DC power, catering to diverse industrial needs based on power requirements.</p></p>
