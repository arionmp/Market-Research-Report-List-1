There are two main types in the market: detachable retrieval bags and non-detachable retrieval bags. </p><p>Detachable retrieval bags allow for easy removal of the specimen without having to remove the entire bag from the body cavity. Non-detachable retrieval bags are used for single-use purposes and are typically disposed of after each procedure. Both types are essential tools for surgeons performing laparoscopic surgeries.</p></p>
