<p><p>Rotary piling rigs are heavy machinery used in civil engineering and construction projects to create piles or deep foundations for structures. These rigs use a rotating drilling tool called a auger or drill bit to bore into the ground and remove soil, while simultaneously inserting piles made of concrete or steel into the drilled holes. They are known for their efficiency and ability to handle various soil conditions.</p><p>The global rotary piling rigs market is projected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
