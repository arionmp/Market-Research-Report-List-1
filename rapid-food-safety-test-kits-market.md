<p><ul><li>Immunoassay Test</li><li>Hybrid Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rapid food safety test kits are available in two main types: immunoassay test and hybrid test. Immunoassay tests detect the presence of specific pathogens or toxins in food samples by utilizing antibodies that bind to the target analytes. On the other hand, hybrid tests combine immunoassays with molecular techniques to simultaneously identify and quantify the target analytes, providing more accurate results. Both types offer fast and reliable food safety testing, helping to ensure the quality and safety of food products.</p></p>
