<p><p>The semiconductor wafer bonding equipment market consists of two main types: wire bonder and die bonder. Wire bonders are used to connect the semiconductor chip to the package by attaching fine wires. On the other hand, die bonders are used to accurately place and align the semiconductor chip onto the substrate or package. Both types of equipment play crucial roles in the semiconductor manufacturing process, ensuring efficient and reliable bonding of semiconductor components, ultimately leading to the production of high-quality electronic devices.</p></p>
