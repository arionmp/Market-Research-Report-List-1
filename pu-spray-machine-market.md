<p><p>PU spray machines are used for applying polyurethane foam insulation or coating on different surfaces. There are two main types of PU spray machines: manual and automatic. Manual machines require the operator to control the spraying process manually, adjusting the flow, pressure, and direction of the spray. On the other hand, automatic machines have advanced technology that allows for automated spraying, with settings programmed beforehand. While manual machines are more affordable and suitable for small projects, automatic machines are preferred for large-scale operations due to their efficiency and precision.</p></p>
