<p><p>The endovascular stent grafts market is classified into three main types: synthetic, biological, and biosynthetic. Synthetic stent grafts are made of artificial materials and are commonly used for their durability and long-term effectiveness. Biological stent grafts are derived from natural tissues, such as human or animal tissues, offering a more biocompatible solution. Biosynthetic stent grafts combine both synthetic and biological materials, providing a hybrid approach that combines the advantages of both types. These stent grafts play a vital role in treating various cardiovascular diseases and are tailored to meet individual patient needs.</p></p>
