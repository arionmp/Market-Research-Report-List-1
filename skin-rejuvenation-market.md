<p><p>The skin rejuvenation market is divided into several types based on the devices used. Energy-based devices utilize different forms of energy, such as light or radiofrequency, to stimulate collagen production and improve skin appearance. Laser-based devices use concentrated light beams to target specific skin concerns. RF devices employ radiofrequency waves to heat the skin and stimulate collagen. Ultrasound devices use sound waves to rejuvenate the skin. IPL devices use intense pulsed light to treat various skin conditions. LED devices emit light at specific wavelengths to improve skin texture and reduce wrinkles.</p></p>
