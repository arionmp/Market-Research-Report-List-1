<p><p>Podiatry lasers are advanced technology devices used in the medical field for various foot-related treatments. There are three main types of podiatry lasers: CO2, Nd:YAG, and Er:YAG. The CO2 laser is primarily used for soft tissue procedures due to its precise and controlled nature. Nd:YAG lasers are effective in targeting deeper tissues and can be used for both soft tissue and vascular treatments. Er:YAG lasers have high absorption rates in water, making them suitable for precise and gentle tissue ablation. Each type offers specific benefits based on their different laser properties.</p></p>
