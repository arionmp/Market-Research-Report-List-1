<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier Night Vision</li><li>Thermal Image Night Vision</li><li>Digital Night Vision</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underwater night vision market includes three main types: image intensifier night vision, thermal image night vision, and digital night vision. Image intensifier night vision uses technology to amplify existing light, allowing for enhanced visibility in dark underwater environments. Thermal image night vision captures the heat emitted by objects and converts it into an image, providing clear visibility even in complete darkness. Digital night vision uses an electronic image sensor to capture and display images in low-light conditions, enhancing visibility underwater. These advancements in night vision technology have greatly improved underwater exploration and navigation.</p></p>
