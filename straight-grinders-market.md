<p><p>Straight grinders are versatile tools used for grinding, polishing, and shaping materials such as metal, wood, or plastic. There are three main types available in the market: cordless, electric, and pneumatic. Cordless straight grinders are powered by rechargeable batteries, offering flexibility and portability without the need for a power outlet. Electric straight grinders, on the other hand, rely on an electric power source and provide consistent power output. Pneumatic straight grinders require an air compressor to operate, allowing for high power and precise control. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications based on user needs and preferences.</p></p>
