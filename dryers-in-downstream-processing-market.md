<p><p>In downstream processing, dryers play a crucial role in various market types such as cell disruption, solid-liquid separation, concentration, purification by chromatography, and formulation. Cell disruption dryers are used to break down cells and release their contents. Solid-liquid separation dryers are utilized to separate solid particles from a liquid solution. Concentration dryers evaporate water or solvents to increase the concentration of a desired product. Purification by chromatography dryers are used to isolate and purify specific compounds. Formulation dryers help in creating final products by blending different ingredients.</p></p>
