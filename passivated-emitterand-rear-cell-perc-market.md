<p><p>The Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell (PERC) technology has two market types: Single PERC and Double Sides PERC. Single PERC refers to solar cells that only have a rear contact layer, allowing light to pass through the front surface for absorption. Double Sides PERC, on the other hand, has rear and front contact layers, which enable it to absorb light from both sides. The main difference lies in the number of contact layers, affecting the overall efficiency and performance of the solar cells.</p></p>
