<p><p>The flame retardant fabric market consists of two main types: inherent flame retardant fabric and treated flame retardant fabric. Inherent flame retardant fabric refers to fabrics that are made with fibers that have flame retardant properties naturally embedded in them. These fabrics do not need any additional treatments to achieve flame resistance. On the other hand, treated flame retardant fabric refers to fabrics that are treated with chemical additives to enhance their flame resistance. These treatments can be applied to various types of fabrics to make them fire-resistant.</p></p>
