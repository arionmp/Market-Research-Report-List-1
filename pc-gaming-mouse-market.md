<p><p>The PC gaming mouse market is divided into two main types: wireless mice and wired mice. Wireless mice use a wireless connection to connect to the computer, providing freedom of movement and eliminating the tangling of wires. They are powered by either built-in rechargeable batteries or replaceable ones. On the other hand, wired mice rely on a physical connection via a USB or PS/2 port. They do not require battery charging but restrict movement due to the wire. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages, and gamers choose based on their preferences and gaming needs.</p></p>
