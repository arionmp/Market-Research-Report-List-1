<p><ul><li>Soluble Butadiene Rubber</li><li>Latex Polybutadiene Rubber</li><li>Bulk Polymerized Sodium Butadiene Rubber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid Nd-BR market consists of three main types: Soluble Butadiene Rubber, Latex Polybutadiene Rubber, and Bulk Polymerized Sodium Butadiene Rubber. Soluble Butadiene Rubber is a solution of high molecular weight polybutadiene in a solvent, used for tire production. Latex Polybutadiene Rubber is a water-based dispersion of polybutadiene, commonly used in gloves and adhesives. Bulk Polymerized Sodium Butadiene Rubber is a solid form of polybutadiene produced through bulk polymerization, used in various industrial applications due to its high abrasion resistance and low temperature flexibility.</p></p>
