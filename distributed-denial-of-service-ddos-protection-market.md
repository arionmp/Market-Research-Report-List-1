<p><p>Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS) protection refers to the range of security solutions and services designed to defend against and mitigate the impact of DDoS attacks. DDoS attacks are a type of cyber threat where multiple compromised systems flood the target with an overwhelming amount of traffic or requests, rendering the system or network inaccessible to legitimate users.</p><p>DDoS protection solutions work by using various techniques to identify and filter out malicious traffic, including rate limiting, traffic analysis, anomaly detection, and behavioral analysis. These solutions can be deployed on-premises, in the cloud, or as a hybrid model, depending on the organization's requirements.</p><p>The DDoS protection market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing frequency and complexity of DDoS attacks. Factors such as the rising adoption of cloud-based services, the proliferation of connected devices, and the growing reliance on digital platforms are driving the demand for robust DDoS protection solutions.</p><p>The market is witnessing a shift from traditional hardware-based solutions to software-based solutions, as organizations seek more scalable and flexible protection options. Additionally, the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies in DDoS protection solutions is gaining traction, as it enables better detection and mitigation of sophisticated attacks.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing a rise in demand for managed DDoS protection services, where third-party service providers offer DDoS protection as a service. This allows organizations to outsource their DDoS protection needs to experts and focus on their core business operations.</p><p>Overall, the DDoS protection market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing sophistication of DDoS attacks and the need for robust security measures to safeguard critical digital infrastructure.</p></p>
