<p><p>The market for Modular Substations can be classified into two types: Fixed Modular Substations and Skid/Trailer Mounted Modular Substations. Fixed Modular Substations are permanent structures that are installed on a concrete pad or foundation. They are designed to be stationary and are commonly used for long-term power distribution needs. On the other hand, Skid/Trailer Mounted Modular Substations are mobile units that can be easily transported to different locations. They are suitable for temporary power requirements or situations where mobility is required, such as construction sites or emergency power situations.</p></p>
