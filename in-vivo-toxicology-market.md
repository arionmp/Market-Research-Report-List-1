<p><p>In Vivo Toxicology Market has various types of testing procedures. Chronic toxicity testing involves the long-term exposure of animals to assess the impact of substances over a prolonged period. Sub-chronic toxicity testing examines the effects of substances when exposed for less than 90 days. Sub-acute toxicity testing analyzes the effects occurring within one month of exposure. Acute toxicity testing investigates the immediate adverse effects resulting from a single exposure to a substance. These testing procedures aid in assessing the safety and potential hazards of chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and other substances.</p></p>
