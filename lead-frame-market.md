<p><p>There are two types of Lead Frame markets: Stamping Process Lead Frame and Etching Process Lead Frame. Stamping Process Lead Frame involves the use of a die to create the desired shape of the lead frame through a stamping process. On the other hand, Etching Process Lead Frame is created by using chemicals to selectively remove unwanted metal, resulting in a thinner and more accurate lead frame. Both types serve their purpose in the manufacturing industry, providing options for different applications and requirements.</p></p>
